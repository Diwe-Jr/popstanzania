PURPOSE

Spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the World

Our Vision

A saved and holly society living a hopeful life, joyfully, faithfully and experiencing God's grace.

Our Mission

Transformation of Christians and Non-Christians into real saved life and insipiring society through spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ to the world and practices hhis love.

Our Objectives

To accomplish its Vision, POP+S will carry out the following objectives

  • a. To work with and local, national and international stakeholders, in order to spread gospel all over the world
  • b. To recruit and train the international missionary.
  • c. To dispatch and support the international missionary.
  • d. To collect and provide the information necessary for international mission.
  • e. To publish the printing materials of mission information.
  • f. To establish and operate the educational, medical and have social services projects.
  • g. To conduct different campus that will gather all members of POP+S for training.
  • h. To conduct seminars and gospel outreach mission within and out of the country.
  • i. Other projects necessary for achieving the purpose of the Society.
