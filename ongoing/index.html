Bible Reading Program

Every day we are reading one or more chapters as how we instructed by our Leaders to read those chapters in a selected book from Bible. Every member is responsible to this and in the evening time, a corresponding group makes a clear and brief representation of what they have learnt in a chapter they being study for a whole day.

Fasting & Prayer

Every day there is a dedicated group from all group we have responsible for fasting and praying for all the prayer points given by the Leader. These prayer lasts for 12 hours in fasting state and in the evening, a report of those who participated and others who do not participated is given out
