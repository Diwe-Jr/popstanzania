Structure of Organization

POP+S Tanzania is governed by the POPS Board of Trustees, which oversees all ministry activities. The Board of trustees meet regularly at every three month to discuss matters of the ministry. While the board serves as the primary overseer, it operates through various leadership and organizational channels. The structure of leadership within POP+S organization is as follows:

  • 1: Board of Trustees
  • 2: Executive Council
  • 3: National Leaders
  • 4: Program Coordinators

In addition, we have several committees and departments operating at different leadership levels to ensure the effective implementation of the ministry's programs. These include the Disciplinary Committee, Election Committee, Evangelism Committee, Discipleship and Training Committee, Pastoring and Missionary Department and Others
